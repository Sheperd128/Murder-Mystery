Meet the Executive Team

Eleanor Vance - Chairwoman of the Board

Eleanor brings decades of aggressive financial strategy to Aegis. While her approach to corporate restructuring has been controversial, her ability to liquidate assets and maximize shareholder profits is unmatched. She oversees all offshore accounts and high-level charity acquisitions.

Dr. Evelyn Reed - Chief Medical Officer

Dr. Reed manages the Aegis Biotechnology division, as well as serving as the personal physician to the executive board. Her recent, highly classified research into synthetic enzymes has pushed the boundaries of modern medicine, ensuring our staff operate at peak physical performance.

Marcus Thorne - Chief Technology Officer

Marcus is the architect behind the Aegis Vault system. Paranoid and brilliant, Marcus built a network that is entirely isolated from external threats, ensuring a secure environment with absolutely no backdoor access for unauthorized users. His strict security protocols are the gold standard of the industry.
