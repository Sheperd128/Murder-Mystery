Executive Board

The visionary minds directing the future of global security.

Eleanor Vance - Chairwoman of the Board

Eleanor brings decades of ruthless, aggressive financial strategy to Aegis Industries. While her approach to corporate restructuring and hostile takeovers has been highly controversial among the board, her ability to liquidate assets and maximize shareholder profits is unmatched. Operating largely in the shadows of the financial sector, she oversees all offshore accounts, venture capital acquisitions, and the company's highly secretive charity foundation.

Dr. Evelyn Reed - Chief Medical Officer

Dr. Reed manages the cutting-edge Aegis Biotechnology division while serving as the exclusive personal physician to the executive board. Her recent, highly classified research into synthetic enzymes and chemical half-lives has pushed the boundaries of modern medicine. She maintains absolute medical authority over the facility, managing the health, biological performance, and internal records of all Tier-1 personnel.

Marcus Thorne - Chief Technology Officer

Marcus is the brilliant, notoriously paranoid architect behind the Aegis Vault system. Operating with absolute autonomy, Marcus built a server network that is entirely isolated from external threats, ensuring a secure environment with absolutely no backdoor access for unauthorized users. His strict security protocols and unpredictable late-night hours make him the ultimate gatekeeper of all Aegis intellectual property.
